About Me

Computational Biologist
turned AI Engineer

I'm a computational biologist and deep learning engineer with deep expertise in biomedical image analysis — from histopathology classification to morphological feature extraction at the cellular level.

My research background spans automated staging of seminiferous tubules using CNNs, building rule-based morphological classifiers, and engineering reproducible scientific ML pipelines. I translate complex biology into precise, deployable software.

As a freelancer, I work with biotech startups, academic labs, and technology companies who need AI that actually works in a scientific context — not just demos.
