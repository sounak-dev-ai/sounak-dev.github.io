About

Computational Biologist
turned AI Engineer

I bridge research-grade science and production-grade software.

My background is in computational biology and deep learning applied to histopathology — from automated staging of seminiferous tubules using ResNeXt models, to rule-based morphological classifiers for spermatogenesis step identification. I know what it means to build AI that has to be right, not just impressive.

As a freelancer, I work with biotech startups, academic research labs, and technology companies who need AI built on genuine domain knowledge. I'm comfortable across the full stack — from PyTorch training loops to React dashboards and Dockerised FastAPI deployments.

When I'm not building models, I teach computational biology and AI concepts through online courses and a growing YouTube channel.
